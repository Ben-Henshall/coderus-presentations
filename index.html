The Semantic Web


What is the semantic web?




The idea of linked data, connecting information from many sources

How is the data linked?




Information is linked through relationships (Tuples), which are defined by schemas


Imagine a schema that defines the world of Pizza:



The relationship "Has topping" has to be of form "x Pizza has y topping"

This could be displayed as:
d:pizza s:hasTopping r:pizzaTopping



  • Strictly defined

  • Allows us to infer
