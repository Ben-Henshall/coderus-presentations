Goal-line Technology

What is Goal-line Technology?

Goal-line Technology is a technological method used in the sport of football to determine whether a ball has fully crossed a goal-line.

Objectives of Goal Line Technology

  • Support referees in decision-making
  • Create a fairer game for all involved

Facts about Goal-line technology

  • Referees wear a special watch which shows an alert of GOAL or NO GOAL
  • The alert sent to the watch is encrypted so that nobody can tamper or abuse the system
  • The alert is transmitted to the watch in less than a second after crossing the line

There are two types of technology used

  • Camera-based
  • Magnetic fields

Camera-based

  • Multiple cameras which detect the ball
    • Seven cameras per goal
  • Specific software evaluates footage from the cameras
  • Makes a decision on whether the ball has entirely crossed the line
  • Stores flightpath data which can be used in replays

Magnetic Fields

  • Electronic circuit embedded in the football
  • Cables are placed underground around the goal and through the goal frame
  • The cables create a low frequency magnetic field
  • Software can calculate exact position of the ball
