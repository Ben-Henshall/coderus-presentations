UPnP AV

What is UPnP

A set of networking protocols to allow devices to discover and interact with eachother

What is UPnP AV?

An extension to UPnP which adds support for devices such as TVs, stereos, MP3 or CD players and even picture frames.

What's the problem being solved?

  • You have all your music and videos on your computer
  • You have your nice screen and speakers in your living room
  • How do I get my music and videos to play on my TV?

What makes up a UPnP AV System?

  • MediaServer
  • MediaRenderer
  • ControlPoint

How does that fit into the example?

  • MediaServer: Computer
  • MediaRenderer: TV
  • ControlPoint: TV or Computer or Something Else

What are the problems with UPnP AV?

  • Multicast
  • Very generic, implementers have to make lots of choices

How is it different to DLNA?

  • DLNA is derived from UPnP AV
  • A smaller subset of functionality, with less flexibility
